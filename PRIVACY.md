# Privacy Policy — Tabbler

Tabbler does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any user data.

- It reads open tabs' URLs and titles using Chrome's `tabs` API, entirely on-device, only to detect and close duplicate tabs.
- Nothing is sent to any server. The extension makes no network requests at all.
- No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.
- No data is written to disk or synced anywhere — there is nothing to delete because nothing is ever stored.
